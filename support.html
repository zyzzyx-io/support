Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my game progress?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you've deleted and reinstalled the app, unfortunately, your progress cannot be recovered at this time. We're working on cloud save functionality for future updates.

The game is crashing. What should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Make sure you have the latest version of the game. 2) Restart your device. 3) Ensure you have enough storage space. If the issue persists, please contact our support with details about your device and the crash.

How can I request a refund for an in-app purchase?

All purchases are processed through Apple. To request a refund, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly.

Is Zyzzyx available on Android?

Currently, Zyzzyx is only available on iOS. We're exploring the possibility of an Android release in the future. Stay tuned!

How do I turn off sound/music in the game?

You can adjust sound and music settings from the in-game settings menu, accessible from the main menu or pause screen.
